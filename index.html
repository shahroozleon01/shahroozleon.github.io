EngineX is made of more than 80'000 lines of code all written by me in the spawn of four years in my free time. Everything made from ground zero empty C++ project with zero project dependencies. Only ImGui (for graphical user interface) and Asset importer open source libraries are used in this project; everything else is made by me. (Also no AI coding tools or any other kinds of it were used in entirety of my projects.)
If in your EngineX experience you are facing low frame rates simply reduce the graphics setting, namely SSAA from default x4 to x1, to have a smoother experience.
Tested on multiple PCs, it should work perfectly fine and fast even on lower end graphics card, if youre experiencing issues consider re-installing your graphics driver for your "dedicated" graphihcs card only, or contanct me for help.
Raytracing only works on compatible graphics cards.
